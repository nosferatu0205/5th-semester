Eigibility Criteria

    External Candidates (candidates outside IIT) must fulfill the following criteria:
  • The candidate must have an undergraduate degree in Software Engineering or Computer Science or Equivalent
  • THe candidate must have CGPA not less than 3,25 on a scale of 4 in higher undergraduate degree
  • Candidates must have GPA 3.5 out of 5 in SSC and HSC or equivalent examinations and total GPA of HSC and SSC must add up to 8 at least. Anyone with grades not on the scale of 5 should contact IIT office
  • Candidates with CGPA not on 4 must contact IIT office
